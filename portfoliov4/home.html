Home

I am an audio engineer, producer, and sound designer based in the Chicago area currently attending Illinois State University. Previously, I was a student at Belmont University in Nashville where I found my passion for audio and sound design for film. Working in Belmont's Columbia Studio A, I gained great experience working in the analog realm on a 1972 API console and an old Neve console.

Currently, I am mixing, mastering, and producing three bands in the Bloomington, IL area and have other artists that I work with remotely from the Chicago and Nashville area.
